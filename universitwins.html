Goal

The UNIVERSITWINS project aims at bias-controlled interpretations of astronomical observations by building digital twins of our cosmic environment. By leveraging advanced techniques like artificial intelligence, it will investigate whether mismatches between observations and models stem from errors or new physics, while setting new digital twin standards for academic and industrial applications.
